## About
**R** means 'red';
**G** means 'green';
**B** means 'blue'.

Your goal is to guess what color is coded behind RGB and click corresponding box.
Each of three colors may be from 0 to 255.
Few examples: 
- RGB(0, 0, 0) is black,
- RGB(255, 255, 255) is white,
- RGB(255, 0, 0) is totally red color.

Good luck!
